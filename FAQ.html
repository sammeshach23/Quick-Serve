Quick Serve

1. How do I place an order?

You can use our quick serve website for ordering your food. Step -1: Open quick serve website
Step -2: Log in/sign up your account
Step -3: Add your orders in the home page.And select order option
Step -4: You can do gpay/pay tm from payment option
Step -5: Your order is successfully send to amenity

2. What payment methods are accepted?

Gpay and paytm are accepted . If it is not possible you can do spot payment also.

3. How long does serving take?

Since you have registered already from our app you can show your token number and receive your food immediately.

4. Is there a minimum order amount?

Yes , there is good news for you , as per quantity of food ordering the total gets reduced.

5.Is there any special food updates?

The special food arrangements with duration ,cost and new menu will be updated from the amenity.
